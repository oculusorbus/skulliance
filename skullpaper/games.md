# Games

## Overview

Both Drop Ship and Oculus Lounge interface with the Skulliance staking platform to reward participants with off-chain points and a fun, interactive gamified experience. Participants connect their wallet to the game website, load in their qualifying NFTs, and embark on immersive gamified raffle experiences.

## Cadence

Games run weekly every Thursday at 9pm UTC. The closure and initiation of games is executed automatically via a script, so there is no risk of delays or weeks off from gameplay.

## Rewards

The top 3 participants on the leaderboard automatically receive a deposit of 1K, 500, and 250 points respectively into their Skulliance staking platform balances for Ohh Meed and/or Disco Solaris. Runner-ups receive in-game points that can be used to purchase consumables. Winners do not receive in-game points, which gives runner-ups a better shot of winning future rounds.

## Gameplay

Participants send in their NFTs in squads of up to 4. If every trait within the game is represented in the combination of NFTs, the player gets the maximum allocation of probability. Players progress through various scenes that feature animated artwork, music, and consequences based on their NFTs and consumables.

## Consumables

Participants earn in-game points that they can use to purchase consumables such as wearables, weapons, and items that can be deployed to improve their chances of a higher score. XP level determines which items are unlocked. Only 1 item can be utilized for each category of items.

## 1v1 Battles

Participants can also opt to create battles and wager points on the battle. If an opponent accepts a battle, the winner receives the wager of points contributed by both participants. 1v1 Battles do not disrupt single player games and can be initiated at any time.

## Leaderboards

There are leaderboards for the current game, all time high scores, and XP earned which also features an instant replay button to view any game logged by a participant.

## Transaction History

Games also include a detailed transaction history detailing points awarded and points spent on consumables.

## Achievements

As players reach milestones such as advancing to certain scenes within game, amassing certain levels of points, and utilizing various consumables for the first time, achievements are unlocked detailing their progression and prestige within the game.
