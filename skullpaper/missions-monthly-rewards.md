# Monthly Rewards

## Monthly Leaderboard

There is a monthly leaderboard for missions participants linked under leaderboards OR under missions stats and you can see your projected rewards in CARBON.

## CARBON Rewards

Every single participant in missions will receive CARBON which can be burned in multiples of 100, in minimum batches of 1,000, to form DIAMOND.

DIAMOND can only be acquired from holding a Diamond Skull NFT, delegating to Diamond Skulls to earn CARBON, earning random DIAMOND from claiming daily rewards, crafting DIAMOND from holding ALL core projects, and now participating in Missions to earn CARBON and burn for DIAMOND.

DIAMOND can be used to purchase ANY NFT in the staking store. It is used to purchase core project rewards at a discount and partner project rewards at a premium.

## Rewards Distribution

The #1 top ranked monthly missions participant will earn 100,000 CARBON which can be burned to form 1,000 DIAMOND.

Subsequent ranks divide 100,000 by their rank. For example, rank 2 would get 50,000 CARBON (100K / 2) and rank 3 would get 33,333 CARBON (100K / 3) and so on and so forth for the entire leaderboard.

Rankings are determined by a calculated score derived from a formula that is based on total missions, successes, failures, and missions in progress.

## Rewards Automation

This initial rewards breakdown creates a fair and generous rewards system for missions participants that will be completely automated every month.

Please note that the criteria for climbing the missions leaderboard and the nature of missions rewards may change in the future.
