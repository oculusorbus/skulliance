# Collections

The Collections page is the registry of every NFT collection Skulliance supports - each identified by its Cardano policy.

## What It Shows

* Every supported collection policy and the **project** it belongs to.
* Filtering by project.
* Each collection's reward rate, which drives nightly staking yield.

Collections are the link between an on-chain NFT and its in-platform identity: when you stake, the platform matches your NFT's policy to a collection here to determine its project, rewards, and eligibility.
