# Merch

Merch turns your NFT art into real, print-on-demand products through a **Printful** integration. Connect a Printful account, pick an eligible NFT, choose product types, and Skulliance handles the image upload and product creation.

## Eligibility

You can only create merch from NFTs in collections that are **merch-licensed**. This ensures only artwork cleared for print-on-demand can be put on products.

## How It Works

1. **Connect Printful** — authorize your Printful account via OAuth.
2. **Pick an NFT** from a merch-licensed collection you own.
3. **Choose product types** — t-shirts, mugs, and other Printful products.
4. **Publish** — Skulliance pulls your NFT's art, uploads it to Printful, generates product mockups, and publishes to your connected store.

## Listing Fee

Each listing charges a **fee in project points**, set per project (default **100 points per product type**). Listing across multiple product types multiplies the fee accordingly, and you must have enough points to cover the total before publishing. The fee is recorded in your [[platform-transactions]] ledger.

## Managing Products

Listed products can be archived and re-listed. Product status tracks as active, archived, or pending.
