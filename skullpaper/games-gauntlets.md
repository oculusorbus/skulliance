# Gauntlets

Gauntlets is an **NFT roguelike**. Each run, you draw a hand of your NFTs and battle through encounters using a project-versus-project weakness system. String together three wins without a loss for a **sweep**.

## The Run

* Each run draws a **hand of 6** eligible NFTs from your collection.
* Pick an NFT to send into each encounter against an opponent NFT.
* Win and you continue; **3 wins without a loss = a sweep** (the best possible result).
* A loss ends the run.
* NFTs you've already played in an unrewarded run are excluded from future hands that week, so you rotate through your collection.

## Matchups (Weakness Chain)

Win chance is driven by a circular weakness chain among the six core projects:

> **6 beats 1 · 5 beats 2 · 4 beats 3 · 2 beats 4 · 1 beats 5 · 3 beats 6**

* **Strong matchup** (you beat the opponent's project): **70%** win chance.
* **Weak matchup** (opponent beats yours): **30%** win chance.
* **Neutral** (same project, or a Diamond Skull is involved): **50%**.
* **Partner NFTs** act as wildcards, mapping to a random core project for the matchup.

## Consumables

Before an encounter resolves, you can play consumables (see [[missions-consumable-items]]):

* **100% / 75% / 50% / 25% Success** add **+4% / +3% / +2% / +1%** win chance.
* **Fast Forward** swaps your chosen NFT for another card in your hand.
* **Double Rewards** doubles the points reward for that encounter.
* **Random Reward** redirects the encounter's reward to a random project's points.

## Scoring & Rewards

* Each win pays **100 points**.
* The **weekly leaderboard** ranks by **sweeps first, then total wins, then fewest losses**.
* The weekly pool is **25,000 CARBON**, divided down the rankings by rank and convertible to DIAMOND at 100:1. See [[staking-crafting]].
