# Boss Battles

Boss Battles are co-op PvE encounters built on the Monstrocity combat engine. Instead of an AI duel, the whole community chips away at shared **bosses** with persistent health, and you're rewarded for the **damage you deal**.

## Playing

* You can only fight bosses tied to **NFT collections you own** - boss eligibility is gated by collection policy.
* Each encounter starts your health fresh, but the **boss's health persists** across all players and all of your attempts. Damage you deal carries over toward defeating it.
* Combat uses the same turn-based match-3 system as the Monstrocity campaign. See [[games-monstrocity]].

## Damage & Bounties

* Your leaderboard metric is **total damage dealt** across all encounters - you're rewarded for chipping away even if you don't land the killing blow.
* A boss carries a **bounty** scaled by a **participation multiplier** (more unique players engaging a boss raises the bounty), so popular bosses pay out more.

## Leaderboard & Rewards

Boss Battles run on a **weekly** cadence. The weekly leaderboard ranks players by total damage dealt and distributes a pool of **CLAW + CARBON** down the leaderboard by rank. CARBON can be burned for DIAMOND - see [[staking-crafting]].

## Consumables

Encounters can use the shared platform consumables (success boosts, Fast Forward, Double Rewards, Random Reward) - see [[missions-consumable-items]].
