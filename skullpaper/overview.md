# Overview

![Skulliance](https://www.skulliance.io/staking/images/skulliance-group.jpg)

## Mission

The mission of Skulliance is to connect skull art investors with the premier skull NFT artists on the Cardano blockchain and elevate the collective art form and community within the space.

![Founding Artists](https://www.skulliance.io/staking/images/og.jpg)

## Founding Artists

The following artists specialize in skull art on Cardano and came together to form Skulliance under the leadership of Oculus Orbus, an avid skull NFT collector and developer. Oculus Orbus developed the Skulliance staking platform that allows holders of NFTs from these founding artists to stake their collections and earn nightly off-chain points that can be redeemed for exclusive incentives.
