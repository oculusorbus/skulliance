# Platform

Beyond staking and games, Skulliance gives you a full set of tools to manage your NFTs, wallets, and stats:

* **[[platform-dashboard]]** - your staked NFT portfolio.
* **[[platform-gallery]]** - discover NFTs and on-platform activity.
* **[[platform-collections]]** - the registry of supported NFT collections.
* **[[platform-leaderboards]]** - competitive rankings across every game mode.
* **[[platform-analytics]]** - personal stats and trends.
* **[[platform-profile]]** - your public achievement profile.
* **[[platform-wallets]]** - connect and manage multiple wallets.
* **[[platform-transactions]]** - your complete points ledger.

These tools are available across the staking platform's navigation and tie together everything you do - staking, missions, realms, games, and the marketplace.
