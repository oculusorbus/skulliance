# Staking

## Overview

The Skulliance staking platform allows holders of qualifying core and partner NFTs the ability to accrue off-chain points that can be redeemed in the staking store for exclusive incentives.

![Skulliance staking dashboard](https://www.skulliance.io/staking/images/screenshots/dashboard.png)

## Membership Requirements

Base membership is not required to participate in staking and earn off-chain points but it is required to claim incentives from the staking store.

## Core Artist Points

## Diamond

DIAMOND is the premier point within the Skulliance ecosystem and can be utilized to claim any incentive from the staking store, either at a discount for core artist offerings or a premium for partner offerings.

### Crafting

Elite members who own NFTs from all of the core artists can convert equal parts of all points to form DIAMOND.

### Shattering

Crafting DIAMOND can be reversed by shattering DIAMOND which converts DIAMOND to equal parts of all core artist points.

## Leaderboards

Skulliance provides an overall leaderboard as well as individual leaderboards for founding artists and partner projects. There is also an all time high leaderboard for missions as well as a monthly leaderboard for missions.

![Leaderboards](https://www.skulliance.io/staking/images/screenshots/leaderboard.png)

## Staking Store

Stakers can use their points to redeem exclusive incentives from the staking store. DIAMOND can be utilized to claim ANY item from the staking store either at a discount for core offerings or at a premium for partner offerings.

![Staking Store](https://www.skulliance.io/staking/images/screenshots/store.png)
