# Factions

## Overview

Factions can be joined by Realm owners to coalesce around their favorite project in Skulliance.

## Stats

Faction stats highlight how well all the members of the Faction are collectively performing in Raids. There is monthly stats as well as all time stats with links to the Factions leaderboards.

## Listing

Factions are listed within the Raid info on the Realms listing. They include the points icon and name of the project associated with the Faction so that participants can easily identify who is aligned with which Faction when determining their raiding strategy.

## Leaderboards

The Factions monthly leaderboard showcases which projects on Skulliance are performing the best in Raids for the current month. It highlights how many project points will be distributed to the members of each Faction at the end of the month. There is also an all time leaderboard to see which Faction has historically performed the best at Raids.
