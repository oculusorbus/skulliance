# Oculus Lounge

![Oculus Lounge Logo](https://www.oculuslounge.vip/lounge/oculus-lounge-logo.png)

## Background

[Oculus Lounge](https://www.oculuslounge.vip) is the premier topless night club in Neo Miami on the Planet Moebius-9. It is a part of the Disco Solaris Universe and was developed by Oculus Orbus.

![Oculus Lounge](https://www.oculuslounge.vip/lounge/oculus-lounge-dancers.png)

## Overview

The Oculus Lounge game allows VIP token holders to send their OG Disco Solaris NFTs to the club with a combination of clothing, toys, and items.

## Rewards

* **TIDDIES** - in-game points rewarded to runner-ups that don't finish in the top 3 on the leaderboard
  * Amount is based on the participant's score
* **MOON** - Skulliance staking platform points for Disco Solaris awarded to the top 3 winners
  * 1K, 500, 250 MOON

## Squads/Consumables

Send OG Disco Solaris NFTs in squads of up to 4 into Oculus Lounge and browse consumables that can be purchased to improve probabilities of getting higher scores. A squad with all clothing, toys, and item types is considered a VIP Squad and has the best probability within the game.

![Squads/Consumables](https://www.oculuslounge.vip/game/game2.png)
