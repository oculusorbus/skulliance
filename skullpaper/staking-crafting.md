# Crafting

Crafting is how you convert between points in Skulliance. It revolves around **DIAMOND** - forming it, breaking it apart, and minting it from CARBON. The Crafting panel lives in the sidebar of the Dashboard and Store pages.

## Craft (Core Points → DIAMOND)

Members who hold all six core artist projects can **craft DIAMOND** by burning **equal parts of all six core points**. The ratio is **1:1 per point type** - crafting 1 DIAMOND consumes 1 of each core point (CRYPT, CYBER, SINDER, HYPE, DREAD, STAR).

## Shatter (DIAMOND → Core Points)

Crafting is reversible. **Shattering** DIAMOND converts it back into **equal parts of all six core points** - 1 DIAMOND becomes 1 of each core point. This is the key move for Realms players: shatter DIAMOND into the exact core points you need to upgrade locations.

## Burn (CARBON → DIAMOND)

CARBON is converted to DIAMOND by **burning in multiples of 100**, at a rate of **100 CARBON = 1 DIAMOND**. CARBON comes from Missions, Realms (your Mine and the raid/faction leaderboards), and Diamond Skull delegation.

## The Crafting Loop

These three operations form the economic engine of the platform:

1. **Earn CARBON** from Missions, Realms, and delegation.
2. **Burn** CARBON into DIAMOND (100:1).
3. **Shatter** DIAMOND into equal parts core points.
4. **Spend** core points on Realms upgrades - or save DIAMOND to claim premium store items.

Because DIAMOND can be both crafted from and shattered into core points, it acts as a universal reserve: convert surplus core points into DIAMOND for storage or store claims, and shatter DIAMOND back whenever you need specific core points.
