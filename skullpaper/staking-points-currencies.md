# Points & Currencies

Skulliance runs entirely on **off-chain points**. Every project has its own point currency, and a few special currencies (DIAMOND, CARBON, CLAW) tie the whole ecosystem together. Points are never on-chain tokens — they live in your platform balance and are spent on store claims, Realms upgrades, marketplace activity, and more.

## Special Currencies

* **DIAMOND** — the premier currency. Can claim **any** item in the store: core offerings at a discount, partner offerings at a premium. Acquired by holding Diamond Skulls, delegating to them, crafting from core points, random daily-reward drops, or burning CARBON. See [[diamond-skulls]].
* **CARBON** — the "fuel" currency. Earned from Missions, Realms (Mine + raid/faction leaderboards), and Diamond Skull delegation. Burn **100 CARBON → 1 DIAMOND**. See [[staking-crafting]].
* **CLAW** — awarded by the Monstrocity game leaderboards. See [[games-monstrocity]].

## Earning Points

* **Nightly staking rewards** are issued to all staked NFTs (core and partner) at 2am CST.
* **Daily rewards** add RANDOM points plus a consumable on a streak. See [[staking-daily-rewards]].
* **Missions, Realms, and Games** award points and CARBON/CLAW through their own systems.

## Spending Points

Points are spent on the [[marketplace-store]] (claims), [[realms-buildings]] (upgrades), [[marketplace-auctions]] and [[marketplace-raffles]] (bids/tickets), and [[marketplace-merch]] (listing fees). Every credit and debit is recorded in your [[platform-transactions]] ledger.
