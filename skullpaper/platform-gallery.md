# Gallery

The Gallery is a discovery wall of NFTs from across the Skulliance community, highlighting what's actively in play.

## What It Shows

* A rotating pool of NFTs from **public, active** stakers, plus your own full collection when you're logged in.
* Activity indicators layered onto each NFT: whether it's currently on a **mission or raid**, or **delegated to a Diamond Skull**.
* Only NFTs from stakers who've set their profile to visible appear publicly (see [[platform-dashboard]] for the visibility setting).

The Gallery is a good way to see which collections are active, spot Diamond Skull delegations, and browse the breadth of art across the ecosystem.
