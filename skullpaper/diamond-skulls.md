# Diamond Skulls

![Diamond Skulls](https://www.skulliance.io/staking/images/projects/skulliance.jpg)

## Collection

Both members AND elite members qualified to mint a Diamond Skull NFT solidifying them within the Inner Circle of the Skulliance. There is a fixed supply of 100 diamond skulls.

## Yield

Diamond Skull holders are entitled to 3 DIAMOND per day for each Diamond Skull they own. They earn 6 DIAMOND per day when the Skulliverse is activated.

DIAMOND can be crafted by burning an equal amount of ALL core artist points in the Skulliance.

## Claims

Core artist NFTs in the store can be claimed with artist points OR a discounted amount of DIAMOND.
