# Marketplace

The Marketplace is where your points turn into real items and NFTs. It spans three systems:

* **[[marketplace-store]]** - claim exclusive items for free as a member.
* **[[marketplace-auctions]]** - bid on NFTs and assets with your points.
* **[[marketplace-raffles]]** - buy tickets for a chance to win NFTs.

All three run on the same off-chain points you earn from staking and gameplay - see [[staking-points]]. Every purchase, bid, ticket, and fee is recorded in your [[platform-transactions]] ledger.

Creating auctions and raffles, and claiming from the store, generally requires Skulliance **membership** - see [[staking-membership]].
