# Marketplace

The Marketplace is where your points turn into real items and NFTs. It spans four systems:

* **[[marketplace-store]]** — claim exclusive items for free as a member.
* **[[marketplace-auctions]]** — bid on NFTs and assets with your points.
* **[[marketplace-raffles]]** — buy tickets for a chance to win NFTs.
* **[[marketplace-merch]]** — turn your NFT art into print-on-demand merch.

All four run on the same off-chain points you earn from staking and gameplay — see [[staking-points-currencies]]. Every purchase, bid, ticket, and fee is recorded in your [[platform-transactions]] ledger.

Creating auctions and raffles, and claiming from the store, generally requires Skulliance **membership** — see [[staking-membership]].
