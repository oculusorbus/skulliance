# Transactions

The Transactions page is your complete points ledger — every credit and debit across the platform, in one place.

## What It Records

* **Credits** — mission rewards, raid spoils, daily-reward bonuses, leaderboard payouts, and refunds.
* **Debits** — store claims, auction bids, raffle tickets, merch listing fees, crafting, and Realms upgrades.
* **Item transactions** — when you receive or spend an item, the item is shown instead of a currency amount.

## Reading It

Each entry shows the date, time, type (credit/debit), amount, currency icon, and a description. Entries are tagged by context — which mission, raid, location, or gauntlet encounter they came from — and daily-reward bonuses are flagged. The ledger shows your most recent 1,000 transactions.

This is the authoritative record behind your [[platform-analytics]] trends and the source of truth for verifying any reward or charge.
