# Transactions

The Transactions page is your complete points ledger - every credit and debit across the platform, in one place.

## What It Records

* **Credits** - mission rewards, raid spoils, daily-reward bonuses, leaderboard payouts, and refunds.
* **Debits** - store claims, auction bids, raffle tickets, merch listing fees, crafting, and Realms upgrades.
* **Item transactions** - when you receive or spend an item, the item is shown instead of a points amount.

## Reading It

Each entry shows the date, time, type (credit/debit), amount, points icon, and a description. Entries are tagged by context - which mission, raid, location, or gauntlet encounter they came from - and daily-reward bonuses are flagged. The ledger shows your most recent 1,000 transactions.

This is the source of truth for verifying any reward or charge.
